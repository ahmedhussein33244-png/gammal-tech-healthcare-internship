## What is healthcare.gammal.tech?

**healthcare.gammal.tech** is a **preventive longevity system** designed to help people live longer and healthier lives.  
It focuses on **prevention and lifestyle improvement**, not just treating diseases after they occur.

> ⚠️ **Note:** This is **NOT a booking application**.

---

## How the System Works

The platform connects several parts of a healthy lifestyle:

- **Restaurants** – Provide healthier food options.
- **Doctors** – Offer medical guidance and advice.
- **Gyms** – Support physical fitness and wellbeing.
- **Artificial Intelligence** – Analyzes health data and gives personalized recommendations.

Together, these elements guide users toward **healthier daily choices** and **disease prevention**.

---

## Features

- Personalized recommendations based on lifestyle data  
- Integration between nutrition, fitness, and healthcare  
- AI-powered insights to improve longevity  
- Focus on preventive healthcare  

---

## Vision

The vision behind healthcare.gammal.tech is to create a system where **technology, healthcare, nutrition, and fitness work together** to:

1. Prevent diseases  
2. Improve quality of life  
3. Increase life expectancy
